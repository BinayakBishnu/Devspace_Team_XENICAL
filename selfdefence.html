Why Self-Defence Is So Important For Women?

Every day, either on social media or on Television, you hear about women being victimized. There are so many such cases about sexual violence or a random attack by strangers as well as being abused by family members. The world is changing and becoming more unpredictable particularly for women. Hence, self-defence training is one of the most needed skill and a must for every woman’s to-do list. In the crime world, self-defence training is suggested, so that the women can resist any kind of sexual assault as well as random attacks.


Reasons for joining the self-defence Training Program

Even now, women are not able to get equal rights in society. They are restricted to do their "responsibilities". People should come out of these restrictions and limitations. Women fail to protect themselves when they are facing any kind of sexual violence and random attack. The main reason for the failure is fear.

The below mentioned are the reasons for joining the self-defence training program and usage of self-defence weapons.
• The program will improve self-confidence in a woman.
• The training program will keep you safe when you are away from your home
• It will reduce the dependency on others
• The training program will get you a healthy and fit lifestyle due to exercise and physical moves.
• It can handle dangerous and emergency situations.
• You will be physically educated in terms of self-defence and to break the stereotype of "women are weak".


When to Join the Self-defence Program?

The world will cause violence not only for adults but also for young girls. Girls can join the course when they are 11 years of age. Before joining the course, they should have physical fitness and a disciplined mindset. In most cases, the height of the girl is playing an important role in the eligibility criteria of the self-defence training. The girl should be at least 36 inches tall to start and join in the self-defence training. But an adult can join and learn a self-defence program at any time if they are physically fit.


Tips and Tricks for Self-defence

The below mentioned are the tips and tricks of self-defence:
• Women should stay fit enough to run fast
• They should check when walking on the empty road to avoid grouping and chain-snatching.
• It is recommended to keep your purse in front and walk by paying full attention around the road.
• Women should avoid high-heeled footwear when they are walking long distances in unknown places.
• Shout “STOP” in a loud and aggressive voice, when they need help from other people.
